Junior Front-End Developer

Hello, I am Holly Wigmore and in 2024 I started my journey into coding. Having completed courses on FreeCodeCamp and SheCodes I have gained a lot of knowledge in languages including JavaScript and React.Js. I hope to continue learning with the aim to become a Front End Developer.

I have spent a lot of time travelling the World and have lived in various countries; including Italy, France, Spain, Greece, Australia and currently New Zealand. I previously worked in the yachting industry and this is what enabled me to do so much travelling and experience living in so many places, despite not always having a lot of time ashore to explore!

As well as travelling I love to be active and spend a lot of my free time hiking, running and cycling. I have now completed 5 half marathons and 1 full marathon. I am also a qualified yoga instructor, obtaining my qualification in March 2022.
